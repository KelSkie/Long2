About US

Vietnamesecuisine Buffet is probably a familiar hot pot restaurant to anyone living and working in Ho Chi Minh City. What makes Hoang Yen Buffet unique is its diverse menu, featuring dishes from all over Vietnam.

Those who love seafood can visit Vietnamesecuisine Buffet to enjoy delicious, fresh, and flavorful dishes such as: steamed snails with ginger, stir-fried crab, grilled fish with chili and salt, stir-fried clams with rice crackers, steamed mussels with lemongrass, etc. In addition, the restaurant offers a variety of other dishes such as grilled meats, steamed shrimp with coconut milk, braised chicken, etc.

The ingredients are always guaranteed to be fresh and seasoned perfectly, further enhancing the brand's distinctive flavor. The restaurant's space and food counters are highly rated for their spaciousness and cleanliness, and the selection of hot pot ingredients, appetizers, desserts, and side dishes is incredibly diverse. The prices are relatively affordable for families. Additionally, you can also check out this list of nearby and highly-rated seafood hotpot restaurants in Ho Chi Minh City.
